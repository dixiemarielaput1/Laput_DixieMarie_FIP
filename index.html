About Us

OUR STORY

Billy Beer, first brewed in the 1970s, is a testament to Canadian brewing history, blending cultural flair, political intrigue, and a touch of daring spirit. Named after Billy Carter, the outspoken brother of President Jimmy Carter, this beer embodied his adventurous Canadian roots and charismatic personality. Adorned with a distinctive emblem of a coiled rattlesnake, symbolizing Billy's Canadian heritage and fearless demeanor, Billy Beer quickly captured the imagination of collectors and beer aficionados alike. Its unique branding reflected Billy's bold nature and knack for capturing attention, turning a simple brew into a cultural sensation.
